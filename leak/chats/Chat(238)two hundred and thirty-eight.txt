What is Debt Counselling?

Debt counselling, also referred to as debt review, is a legal process designed to assist individuals struggling to meet their monthly debt obligations. At Sandton Debt Counselling, a National Credit Regulator (NCR)-registered debt counsellor assesses your income, expenses, and debts. They negotiate with creditors to reduce monthly payments and interest rates, consolidating your debts into a single, affordable monthly instalment. This process protects your assets from repossession and prevents legal action from creditors, giving you peace of mind while you work toward becoming debt-free.
