Executive Summary: Iskra
Company Status

Iskra is a private, for-profit company.
Founding and Headquarters

Iskra was founded in 2022 and is headquartered in Singapore.
Founders

The company was founded by Hong Kyu Lee, a South Korean entrepreneur with a background in technology and gaming. He holds degrees in computer science and has extensive professional experience in IT startups and blockchain ventures in Asia. His most significant achievements include leading previous tech firms to successful funding rounds and pioneering early Web3 projects that integrated gaming with decentralized technologies, which directly informed Iskra's focus on blockchain gaming ecosystems.

    CEO: Eugene Lee, with a strong background in gaming and blockchain, previously involved in IT and gaming veteran roles.

Product and Services

Iskra offers an all-in-one Web3 gaming platform that integrates blockchain services, including a wallet, decentralized exchange (DEX), NFT marketplace, governance staking, and a suite of Web3 games. The unique innovation is the ActFi (Activity-Focused Incentive) system, which rewards users for contributions to the ecosystem, such as community participation, gameplay, and content creation, using the native $ISK token. This creates a sustainable, community-driven economy beyond traditional play-to-earn models.

The platform helps customers‚Äîgamers and game studios‚Äîoperate more efficiently by providing seamless access to blockchain tools without needing multiple apps, reducing transaction costs through integrated DEX and wallet services, and alleviating pain points like fragmented rewards and lack of ownership by enabling true digital asset ownership and ongoing earnings (even offline in some games). Studios benefit from easier game launches and monetization via Iskra's publishing support.
Market

Target customers include individual gamers seeking rewarding blockchain experiences and game studios looking to launch Web3 titles. The total addressable market (TAM) for blockchain gaming is estimated at USD 12.21 billion in 2024, projected to grow to USD 17.50 billion in 2025, with further expansion driven by increasing adoption of Web3 technologies.

    All-in-One Ecosystem: Combines gaming, wallet, DEX, and governance in a single platform, reducing user friction.

    Community-Centric Model: Rewards contributions beyond gameplay, fostering loyalty and organic growth.

It monetizes by bridging gamers and studios, taking a cut from in-game economies and partnerships.
