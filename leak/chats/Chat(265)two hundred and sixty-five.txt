What Are Exam Dumps?

Exam dumps are collections of questions and answers purportedly taken from previous exams, often shared on websites or forums. They are marketed as a way to practice or memorize questions that may appear on the actual exam. However, their legitimacy, accuracy, and ethical implications vary widely.
