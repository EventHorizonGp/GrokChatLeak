Product and Services: What is the company's product or service, and what is the unique innovation behind it? How does their product help its customers operate more efficiently, cheaply, and with less pain?
Reducto's core product is a Document Ingestion API that processes unstructured documents (e.g., PDFs, images, spreadsheets, slides) into structured, LLM-ready data. The unique innovation is a multi-pass system combining computer vision, OCR, and vision-language models to visually break down documents, correct errors agentically, and interpret content in context for superior accuracy. This helps customers by automating complex data extraction from financial statements, legal docs, and tables, achieving 99%+ accuracy where generic tools hit only 80%. It reduces manual effort, operational costs, and errors, enabling faster AI workflows with enterprise-grade reliability (e.g., SOC2/HIPAA compliance and 99.9% uptime), particularly in high-stakes industries.
